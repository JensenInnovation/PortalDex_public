
PortalDex is an unofficial fan-made collection tracker for Skylanders figures. It is not made by, affiliated with, or endorsed by Activision. If you are looking for the official Skylanders app, please visit Activision's website.

Need help with the app? Contact klaus@jensen-innovation.com
——

Keep track of every Skylanders figure you own. Browse the complete catalog, log your collection, watch your completion percentage grow, and never lose track of what you still need — all offline, no account required.

**Browse the Full Catalog**
The built-in database covers all six Skylanders games and every figure type — characters, traps, vehicles, expansion packs, creation crystals, and trophies. Filter by game, element, rarity, or type. Tap any figure to see its details and add it to your collection or wishlist in one tap.

**Track Your Collection**
Your dashboard shows overall completion as a percentage and breaks progress down by game and by element. Tap any stat to drill into the full list. One tap shows every figure you are still missing. Another shows your duplicates.

**A Fan-Built Database**
307 base figures and 56 named collector variants across all six games:

• Spyro's Adventure
• Giants
• Swap Force
• Trap Team
• SuperChargers
• Imaginators

Collector editions are included — Eon's Elite, Legendary variants, Glow in the Dark, Phantom, and Dark editions. Crossover figures (Donkey Kong, Bowser, Crash Bandicoot, Dr. Neo Cortex) are tagged separately.

**Rarity Tiers**
Figures are tagged by rarity so you can focus on the hard-to-find ones first. The Rare Only filter on the Missing screen shows only Legendary, Elite, and Crossover figures at a glance.

**Duplicate Manager**
Own more than one copy of a figure? The duplicate manager lets you track quantities and decide which extras to trade or sell.

**Wishlist**
Found a figure you still need? Bookmark it to your wishlist in one tap. When you later add it to your collection, it is automatically removed from the wishlist.

**Personal Notes**
Attach free-text notes to any owned figure — where you bought it, its condition, whether it is for trade. Add as many notes as you need.

**Completely Offline**
No account required. No cloud sync. No subscription. Your collection is stored locally on your iPhone and never leaves your device.
