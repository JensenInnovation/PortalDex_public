# Privacy Policy

**PortalDex — Skylanders Collection Tracker**

Effective date: 8 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the PortalDex app ("the app", "we") handles
information. PortalDex is developed by Jensen Innovation (Klaus Jensen).

## Summary

PortalDex does **not** collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data.
There are no user accounts, no advertising, no analytics, and no tracking. Your
collection data stays on your device.

## Information we do NOT collect

- We do not require an account or sign-in.
- We do not collect names, email addresses, location, contacts, or device
  identifiers.
- We do not use advertising or third-party analytics/tracking SDKs.
- Your collection, wishlist, and settings are stored **locally on your device**
  and are never uploaded to us or to any server.

## NFC

The app uses your device's NFC reader solely to read Skylanders figures held
against the device, in order to identify them and add them to your local
collection. NFC data is processed on-device only and is not transmitted
anywhere.

## Optional photo contributions

The app includes an optional "Contribute a photo" feature. If — and only if —
you choose to use it, the app opens an external **Google Form** in your browser
where you may voluntarily submit a photo of a figure and, optionally, a name to
credit the photo. This is entirely optional and is never required to use the
app.

Any information you enter in that form is submitted to and processed by Google
Forms, and is used only to improve the figure images shown in the app. Google's
handling of that data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy
(https://policies.google.com/privacy). Do not submit personal or sensitive
information in the form.

## Links to other services

The app can open third-party websites (for example eBay, Amazon, and
PriceCharting) in your browser so you can search for figures. Those websites are
operated by third parties and have their own privacy policies. We do not control
and are not responsible for their content or practices.

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone,
including children under the age of 13.
